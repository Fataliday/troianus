About Us

Troianus Olive Oil LLC is a newly established company with a fresh perspective and strong commitment to quality. We source our olives from the fertile groves of the Aegean region, known for their ideal climate and rich soil.

We take pride in producing authentic extra virgin olive oil using cold-press techniques that preserve the natural flavor, aroma, and health benefits. Our commitment to sustainability ensures that every bottle of Troianus Olive Oil reflects the purity of nature.

With a growing presence in global markets, we aim to share the heritage and rich taste of Turkish olive oil with customers worldwide.

Our Products

Explore our premium line of extra virgin olive oils crafted for every culinary need. Whether you're looking for bulk options for foodservice or elegantly bottled selections for retail, Troianus offers quality you can trust.

Our oils are rich in antioxidants and nutrients, making them ideal for salads, cooking, and gourmet dishes. Available in various packaging sizes and customizable labeling for business partners.
